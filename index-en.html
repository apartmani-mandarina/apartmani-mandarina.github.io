Welcome to Apartmani Mandarina

Our charming complex is located in the beautiful coastal town of Podaca. Here you can enjoy the relaxed atmosphere and experience warm hospitality. We offer you three modern and bright accommodation units: two spacious apartments and a cozy studio. All accommodations are comfortably furnished and perfect for a relaxing vacation. Podaca is ideal for beach lovers and adventurers. Whether you want to relax on the beach, explore nature or experience the culture, you will find everything you need here. We look forward to welcoming you.

Apartments

Apartment 1

This apartment has two cozy bedrooms and a bathroom. The spacious kitchen is fully equipped with a refrigerator, stove, coffee machine and microwave. On a large sunny balcony with deck chairs and seating you can enjoy the sea view. Additional seating is available on the terrace with an outdoor kitchen. The property offers the use of a gas or charcoal grill. The apartment is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi. Free parking is available on site for this apartment.

  • Dimension 68m2
  • 4 persons
  • 2x bedrooms, 1x bathroom
  • Balcony with sea view
  • Teracce
  • Air conditioning
  • TV
  • WiFi (free)
  • Parking spot (free)

Apartman 2

Ovaj svijetli apartman ima dvije spavaće sobe i jedno kupatilo. Sunčani balkon s garniturom za sjedenje pruža širok pogled na more. Prostrana kuhinja opremljena je hladnjakom i štednjakom. Smještaj nudi i korištenje roštilja na plin ili ugljen te garnituru za sjedenje s pogledom na more iza kuće. Apartman je opremljen klima uređajem, TV-om i besplatnim bežičnim internetom. Za ovaj apartman dostupno je besplatno parkiralište u sklopu objekta.
